Bionic Scope

Wearable System for Visual Extension Triggered by Bioelectrical Signal

We can watch from near to distant clearly and seamlessly.

When human intends to move his / her muscles, bioelectrical signal leaks out on the surface of skin.
The user is connected to this system by the signal.

Applications

It is supposed that this system is useful for various activities such as watching sports and birds, rescue, guard, surgery.
